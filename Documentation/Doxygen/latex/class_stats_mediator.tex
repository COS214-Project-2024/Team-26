\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Provides statistical data and detailed information for the city simulation. 

The \doxylink{class_stats_mediator}{Stats\+Mediator} class retrieves various statistics related to population, housing, and resources, as well as comprehensive city details, from government data.
