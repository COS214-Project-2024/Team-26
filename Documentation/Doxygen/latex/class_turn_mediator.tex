\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Manages interactions within the city simulation, including handling population dynamics and government operations. 

The \doxylink{class_turn_mediator}{Turn\+Mediator} class coordinates various aspects of city management in each round of the simulation, such as updating the population, adjusting tax rates, and managing government decisions.
